Paul's Favourite Movies

My top 3 10/10 movies

1. Prison Break

Yes, yes, I know it is a TV series, but I still have to mention it. This has to be the greatest screenplay ever written by mankind! I am not kidding! If you haven't watched it yet, you don't know what you are missing. Add it to your movies watchlist and thank me later!

My favourite characters include:
Wentworth Miller
Robert Knepper (the famous T-Bag!)
Dominic Purcell

2. Home Alone

Brings back the coolest childhood memories. What was Christmas without Home Alone anyway! I would say probably not that merry!

3. Avatar

What can I even say about James Cameron. His movies are a pure touch of gold! Everybody knows that!
